Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2025

VoidSweeper ("the game", "we", "our") is developed by Andre Ribeiro. This policy explains what data is collected when you play VoidSweeper and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data — If you sign in, we collect a user identifier via Firebase Authentication (Google or anonymous sign-in). No passwords are stored by us.
  • Game scores and leaderboard data — Your high scores and sector progress are stored in Google Firestore to power the in-game leaderboard.
  • Analytics — We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data (screen views, session length, feature usage). No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Crash reports — Firebase Crashlytics collects crash logs and device information (OS version, device model) to help us fix bugs.
  • Advertising — We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the leaderboard and save your progress
  • To improve game stability and fix crashes
  • To understand how players use the game (anonymous)
  • To display advertisements

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is shared only with the third-party services listed below, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob — Advertising

4. Data Retention

Leaderboard scores are retained indefinitely. Analytics and crash data are retained per Google's default retention policies. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

5. Children

VoidSweeper is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Depending on your location you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.
